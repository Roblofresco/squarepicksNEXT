## Content Structure

FAQ organized into three categories:

### **General**

**What is SquarePicks?**
- Sweepstakes based on real-world sports scores
- Pick squares on 10x10 grid
- Win if numbers match score digits

**Is SquarePicks considered gambling?**
- No, it's a legal promotional sweepstakes
- All games are promotional sweepstakes
- Winners determined by random chance
- No purchase necessary
- Free entry available
- Equal opportunity for all participants
- Not lotteries, gambling, or games of skill

**Where is SquarePicks legal to play?**
- Available to 21+ residents in specific US states
- Where sweepstakes are permitted by law
- Void where prohibited
- Automatic location checking

---

### **Gameplay & Rules**

**How are winning numbers chosen?**
- Assigned completely randomly
- AFTER paid board is full or free board closes
- Random assignment for columns (X-axis)
- Random assignment for rows (Y-axis)
- Fair and unpredictable

**What if a game is canceled or postponed?**
- Canceled: All entries voided, fees refunded
- Postponed: Follow official league rulings
- Check board rules or app notifications

**How are prizes distributed?**
- Based on total pot (Entry Fee Ã— 100)
- Four winners for standard boards
- Each gets 20% of pot
- Winners at: Q1, Halftime, Q3, Final
- Credited to account balance

**Can I win more than once?**
- Yes, same square can win multiple periods
- Each period earns separate prize (20% of pot)

---

### **Account & Verification**

**Why verify identity and provide SSN?**
- Required by law and regulations
- Ensures eligibility (age, location)
- Prevents fraud
- Tax reporting compliance (1099-MISC)
- Secure handling per Privacy Policy

**Is my personal information safe?**
- Yes, industry-standard encryption
- Secure data practices
- Not sold/shared except as required by law
- Only shared for payments and verification

**Can I play for free?**
- Yes, one free entry per weekly sweepstakes
- Via AMOE (Alternative Method of Entry)
- Same odds as paid entries
