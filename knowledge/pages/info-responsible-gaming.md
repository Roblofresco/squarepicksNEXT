### **2. Player Eligibility**

**Age Requirement:**
- 21 years or older required
- Verification methods employed

**Location Requirement:**
- Eligible US states only
- Legal sweepstakes participation
- Geolocation technology enforced

---

### **3. Playing Responsibly**

**Responsible Play Tips:**
- **Play for Fun:** Entertainment, not income
- **Set Limits:** Decide time/money beforehand
- **Know the Rules:** Understand odds and payouts
- **Balance Activities:** Don't neglect responsibilities
- **Recognize Warning Signs:**
  - Spending more than intended
  - Chasing losses
  - Borrowing money to play
  - Neglecting responsibilities

---

### **4. Tools to Help Manage Play**

**Available via Profile Settings:**

**Deposit Limits:**
- Voluntary limits on deposit amounts
- Time period options (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Stricter than standard limits

**Entry Limits / Spend Limits:**
- Limit on entry fee spending
- Limit on number of entries
- Time period options available

**Self-Exclusion:**
- Take a break from playing
- Options:
  - 6 months
  - 1 year
  - Longer durations
  - Permanent exclusion
- Effects during exclusion:
  - Account inaccessible
  - No deposits
  - No contest entries
  - Removed from promotions

---

### **5. Where to Find Help**

**24/7 Confidential Resources:**

**National Council on Problem Gambling (NCPG):**
- Website: `https://www.ncpgambling.org`
- Helpline: 1-800-522-4700 (Call or Text)
- Chat: `ncpgambling.org/chat`

**Additional Resources:**
- State-specific resources available
- Depends on jurisdiction
