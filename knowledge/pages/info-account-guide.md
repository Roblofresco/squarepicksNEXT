## Content Structure

The guide covers three main topics:

### 1. **Funding Your Account (Deposits)**
- **Eligibility:** 21+, valid US state, identity verification
- **Payment Method:** PayPal only
  - PayPal balance, linked bank, or debit/credit via PayPal
  - Prepaid gift cards not accepted
- **Deposit Limits:** $500 per 24 hours
- **Confirmation:** Instant for most methods, delayed for eChecks
- **Security:** PayPal handles all payment processing

### 2. **Withdrawing Your Winnings**
- **Verification Required:** Identity + SSN before first withdrawal
- **Eligible Destinations:**
  - Verified PayPal
  - Verified Venmo
  - Verified US bank account
  - Specific debit card
  - NOT credit cards
- **Playthrough Requirement:** Funds must be used for entries before withdrawal
- **Minimum Amount:** $5.00
- **Frequency Limit:** One withdrawal per 24 hours
- **Processing Schedule:** Daily at 2:00 PM ET
- **Taxes:** Form 1099-MISC for net winnings over $600

### 3. **Managing Your Profile**
- Accessing profile via bottom nav or menu
- Information displayed:
  - Username
  - Email
  - Balance
  - Transaction history
  - Links to settings/docs
- Editable information (username, password)
- Contact info maintenance
- Verification status
- Responsible gaming tools
